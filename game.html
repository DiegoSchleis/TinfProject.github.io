The Game(F1 2016)

Published on 19th August 2016, F1 2016 is the latest edition of the F1 game series published and developed by Codemasters. It is obviously based on real life Formula One and is the best F1 simulator ever published. The game has stunning graphics combined with the best and most realistic car handle ever. It also has a very deep "career-mode" that can last over ten years(not real-life years). Additionally F1 2016 has an online mode with matchmaking in case the AI-drivers are too easy for you.
